Mission Statement

ROTC Companion trains, educates, and prepares cadets by giving them quick access to fitness tools, Army knowledge, practical checklists, and training references needed to show up ready.

TRAIN EDUCATE REHEARSE PREPARE

What it does

Built around the main things cadets need: PT, reference material, practice tools, and preparation.

PT

Physical Training

Track fitness and prepare for PT requirements.

  • AFT calculator and event explanation
  • PT tracker for AFTs, rucks, workouts, and PRs
  • AlarmKit PT alarms and event checklists
  • Recovery and workout planning tools
INFO

ROTC Reference

Quick access to common knowledge cadets need during school, labs, and field training.

  • Planning formats like OPORD, WARNO, FRAGO, and TLPs
  • Warrior skills, land navigation, radio etiquette, and reports
  • Uniform guidance and sizing tools
  • Acronyms, ranks, customs, and cadet basics
REP

Practice Tools

Practice information instead of only reading it.

  • Quizzes and flashcards
  • Map plotting and land navigation drills
  • 9-Line and order builders
  • Scenario practice and simulations

Why it exists

ROTC information is often spread across slides, handouts, notes, and cadre guidance. The goal is to make useful information easier to reach.

1

Made for cadets

Designed around the daily things cadets actually use: PT, uniforms, field skills, reports, formations, and preparation.

2

Built to be practical

The app focuses on tools that help cadets act, not just read: calculators, trackers, alarms, checklists, and practice reps.

3

Independent project

ROTC Companion is independently developed and is not an official Army, ROTC, Department of Defense, or government application.
